FAQ 🤔

  • What is Mali Coin? 🤔

    Mali Coin is a government-backed cryptocurrency built on the Solana blockchain. It aims to drive economic growth in Mali, providing financial opportunities and empowerment to its citizens.

  • How do I buy Mali Coin? 💎

    You can purchase Mali Coin on decentralized exchanges (DEX) that support the Solana blockchain, such as Pump.fun to store your tokens securely.

  • What are the tokenomics? 📊

    Mali Coin follows a transparent and fair tokenomics model, with a focus on sustainable growth and community support. Token distribution includes allocations for development, public access, and community incentives.

  • How can I join the community? 👥

    Join our growing community on Twitter and MaliMemeCoin@gmail.com to stay updated on Mali Coin developments, participate in discussions, and connect with like-minded supporters!
